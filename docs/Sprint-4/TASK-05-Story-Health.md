# Vision

Story Health transforms AI Story Studio into an intelligent storytelling assistant.

Instead of only generating stories,

AI evaluates whether the story is actually engaging.

It analyzes

emotion

pacing

logic

character development

story structure

and predicts viewer engagement before production begins.

---

# Goal

Automatically analyze every generated story.

Detect weaknesses.

Suggest improvements.

Predict audience retention.

Increase storytelling quality before any image or video is generated.

---

# Problem

A story can be

well written

grammatically correct

beautifully illustrated

yet still be boring.

Current AI tools only generate text.

They do not understand whether the story is interesting.

---
