# Vision

Dynamic Scene System removes the need for manually selecting scene counts.

Instead of asking

"How many scenes should I create?"

AI decides

How many scenes are needed

Where scenes should begin

Where scenes should end

When visuals should change

How long each scene should remain on screen

The result is a natural cinematic experience.

# Scene Types

AI classifies scenes automatically.

---

Opening Scene

Strong visual hook.

Usually 4–8 seconds.

---

Dialogue Scene

Focused on narration.

Medium duration.

---

Action Scene

Fast transitions.

Short duration.

---

Emotional Scene

Longer pacing.

Less camera movement.

---

Transition Scene

Connects two story sections.

Usually simple visuals.

---

Ending Scene

Strong closing image.

Longer duration.
