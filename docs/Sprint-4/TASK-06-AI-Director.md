# Vision

AI Director is the brain of AI Story Studio.

Instead of simply creating prompts or analyzing videos,

AI becomes the production director.

It continuously evaluates the project and makes professional directing decisions.

The goal is not automation.

The goal is intelligent direction.

AI should think like an experienced film director.

---

# Goal

Continuously monitor every project and make production decisions automatically.

AI decides

• where to change visuals

• when to zoom

• when to use video instead of images

• when pacing becomes slow

• when emotion is missing

• when the audience may lose attention

---

# Problem

Most creators know HOW to create content.

Very few know HOW to direct content.

Questions creators struggle with

Should this be an image?

Should this be a video?

Should the camera move?

Should this scene be shorter?

Should I zoom?

Should I add silence?

Should I change the angle?

AI Director answers these questions.
