## PRODUCT BIBLE
Version: 1.0

---

# Vision

AI Content Studio is an end-to-end AI-powered content production platform.

The goal is to let a creator produce an entire piece of content from a single workspace.

Instead of using multiple AI tools separately, users complete every production step inside one application.

The platform manages:

- Idea
- Script
- Storyboard
- Characters
- Prompts
- AI Images
- Voice
- Video
- Subtitle
- Thumbnail
- Shorts
- Publishing

The product should feel like a professional production studio rather than an AI generator.

---

# Mission

Reduce content production time from days to minutes.

A creator should be able to create professional content without needing technical knowledge.

The platform should automate repetitive work while keeping the creator in control of every creative decision.

---

# Core Philosophy

AI should assist creativity.

AI should never replace creativity.

The user is always the director.

The application is the production team.

- Storyboard generation

The children's story workflow should always receive long-term support.
